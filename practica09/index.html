Workbyworks is a multidisciplinary design studio based
in New York and Shanghai, founded by Han Gao and
Wenbo Yi. Working with clients around the world, they
provide creative direction, branding, visual identities,
packaging, website and book design.
In response to the ever-changing creative design
context, Workbyworks Studio shares flexibility in
projects across a wide range of different fields, the
influence take on various forms at large and small scale.
